What’s new in BMS v24?

  • 💻 Enhanced Granular Permissions in Study Manager: We've introduced a comprehensive set of permissions in the study manager, enabling administrators to have precise control over access to every aspect of studies, down to individual actions. To learn more, click here

  • ⚡ Performance Boost in Advance Process: We've reimagined and optimized the advance process to significantly improve its performance, eliminating previous limitations on the number of lines that could be advanced. Additionally, you can now advance from sub-observation units! Learn more here

  • 🧬 Expanded Gigwa Integration for Genotypic Data: In this release, we've broadened our integration with Gigwa to better support your genotypic data needs. You can now effortlessly import data for specific markers directly into your study for more informed decision-making. Learn more about this feature here

  • 🔌 Comprehensive BrAPI 2.0 Support: We're excited to announce increased support for BrAPI 2.0, extending to even more search services. Discover the full potential of our BrAPI 2.0 support here.

  • 🐸 Let us know what you think! 🐸
    We'd like your feedback! We will be routinely publishing the BMS features under consideration. You can vote, provide insights and submit new ideas in our ProductBoard page
