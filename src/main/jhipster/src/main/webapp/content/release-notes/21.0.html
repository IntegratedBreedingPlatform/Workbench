v21 Release Notes

  • Lot creation: You can now create inventory lots from planted material directly, without generational advancement.
    • Learn more here
  • Coefficient of parentage ( Beta version ): You can now calculate the pedigree coefficient of parentage for a limited set of germplasms. More to come soon!
    • Learn more here
  • Import files associated to environments: You can now upload files and photos associated to the study environments details or conditions.
    • Learn more here
  • Graphical filtering tool: You can now filter by your favorite locations and export transposed versions of your dataset
  • Germplasm descriptors, attributes and names: Support for strings up to 5.000 characters long
  • General bug fixes and performance improvements

We'd like your feedback! We will be routinely publishing the BMS features under consideration. You can vote, provide insights and submit new ideas in our ProductBoard page. Additionally, you can check what's in the roadmap for the next iterations here .
